you continue on, the comforting bump of the sword against your leg eases you.

as you walk, you take in your surroundings.

you can hear animals in the hedges around you.

birds chirp from above you, smaller creatures rustling the leaves by your feet.

you try to recall your past, or at least any events from before you were here.

nothing surfaces in your mind. you cannot recall a single moment from your life before now.

surely you must have had a life before this?

before you can spiral, you come upon a

gate.
